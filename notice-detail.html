Hackathon adventures: Mapping concept to code

25th May, 2024

From crafting dazzling rebrands, expanding our operational horizons, and fine-tuning our services, Leapfrog has evolved progressively throughout the years. In this decade-long journey, we bid adieu to some team members while welcoming new ones joining the fold — change is the influx of momentum.

In 2012, we kicked off our first batch of internships, unsure of what lay ahead. Now, as we have recently welcomed our 46th batch of interns, we reflect on how far we’ve come. To date, we’ve been flooded with thousands of applications, leading us to meticulously handpick 1045+ interns through a rigorous selection process. This internship journey was no more than the Olympics, which holds intensity, excitement, and thoroughness. And guess what? 460+ interns, who impressed us with their coding skills, growth, and hard work, have been converted to full-time positions, as Leapfroggers.

Without further ado, let me tell you that “We are having a code-blooded upgrade to our internship.” Yes, you heard it right. We are leveling up our internship game with “Leapfrog Fellowship.”
